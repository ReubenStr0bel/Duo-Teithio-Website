Duo Teithio

Thom and Reuben met in 2019 during their studies at The Royal Welsh College of Music and Drama in Cardiff. The duoâ€™s repertoire is comprised of a variety of styles and instrumentation, from Baroque and early Classical, to Romantic and contemporary as well as an assortment of Musical Theatre pieces. Being a duo of Multi-Woodwinds & Piano, they explore countless contrasting genres and composers within a single recital. They perform all over the world aboard various cruise lines and in concert series across the UK throughout the year.

Having met in Wales, the duo took their name from a Welsh word for travel: teithio. Much of the music they perform originates from around the world, giving plenty of opportunities to embed an international mindset in their music. From tailoring concert programs to the areas of the world they visit to including indigenous instruments in their recitals, a Duo Teithio concert will take you on a journey like no other. The duo also frequently arrange music for their own purposes, so expect familiar tunes in ways never before heard and to be introduced to music that may just become your new favourite.
