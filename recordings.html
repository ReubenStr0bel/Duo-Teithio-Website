Hear us Perform

Duo Teithio, first and foremost, perform music that draws us together. We see music as a universal language that we all have a part to play in. Alongside our audience, we travel and explore the world we live in through music. As a classical duo we often perform well-known and highly regarded classical music, but we believe that great music can be found anywhere you look and as such we strive to make our concerts varied and unpretentious. Among our list of repertoire you will find folk music, jazz, musical theatre, music from film and much more. Furthermore, we frequently write and arrange our own music to broaden the repertoire available to us. See some snippets of our past concerts here if you would like to get a taste of this first hand.
